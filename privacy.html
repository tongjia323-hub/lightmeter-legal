Light Meter

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

English

Light Meter is a local-first exposure reference app for checking light before photo and video shoots.

Light Meter does not collect data for tracking, does not use advertising tracking, does not include third-party analytics SDKs, and does not upload camera previews, photos, videos, microphone audio, location, account, or tracking data to developer servers.

Camera

Light Meter uses the iPhone camera preview locally to estimate scene brightness, EV, highlight risk, shadow risk, spot readings, center-weighted readings, and average readings. Camera preview analysis happens on device.

When the user taps the photo or cine controls, the app may capture a photo or record a video and save it to the user's Photos library.

Microphone

Microphone access is used only when the user starts video recording, so the recorded video can include audio. Light Meter does not make standalone audio recordings, does not perform speech recognition, and does not upload audio.

Photos

Photo Library add access is used only to save user-initiated photos and videos. Light Meter does not read the user's existing Photos library.

Local Data

  • Calibration offset
  • Meter logs
  • EV, ISO, aperture, shutter, metering mode, highlight ratio, shadow ratio, and reading diagnostics saved in meter logs
  • Trial start time, trial status, purchase status, and reinstall lock status stored locally and in Keychain

This local data is used only for app functionality, the one-day trial, and lifetime unlock status. It is not uploaded to developer servers.

In-App Purchase

Light Meter uses Apple StoreKit for the one-time lifetime unlock. Purchase and restore flows are handled by Apple and the App Store.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, email tongjia323@gmail.com.

中文

测光仪是一款本地优先的拍摄前曝光参考工具，用于在照片和视频拍摄前检查光线。

测光仪不收集用于追踪的数据，不接入广告追踪，不接入第三方分析 SDK，不向开发者服务器上传相机预览、照片、视频、麦克风音频、位置、账号或追踪数据。

相机

测光仪使用 iPhone 相机预览在本机估算画面亮度、EV、高光风险、暗部风险、点测读数、中央重点读数和平均读数。相机预览分析只在设备本机完成。

当用户点击照片或视频测光控件时，App 可能按用户操作拍摄照片或录制视频，并保存到用户的系统相册。

麦克风

麦克风权限仅在用户主动开始视频录制时使用，用于把现场声音写入录制的视频。测光仪不做独立录音、不做语音识别，也不上传音频。

相册

相册添加权限仅用于保存用户主动拍摄的照片和视频。测光仪不读取用户已有照片库内容。

本地数据

  • 校准偏移值
  • 测光记录
  • 测光记录中的 EV、ISO、光圈、快门、测光模式、高光比例、暗部比例和读数诊断
  • 试用开始时间、试用状态、购买状态和重装锁定状态，这些信息保存在本机和 Keychain 中

这些本地数据仅用于 App 功能、1 天试用和终身解锁状态判断，不上传到开发者服务器。

内购

测光仪通过 Apple StoreKit 提供一次性终身解锁。购买和恢复购买流程由 Apple 和 App Store 处理。

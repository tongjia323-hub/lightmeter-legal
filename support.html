What Light Meter Does

  • Live EV reference reading from the iPhone camera preview.
  • Photo metering with ISO, aperture, shutter, and metering mode controls.
  • Cine metering with frame rate, shutter angle, and ND guidance.
  • Light checks for highlight risk, shadow risk, and exposure condition.
  • Manual and auto exposure modes.
  • Meter logs and local report export.

Important Note

Light Meter provides practical exposure reference before shooting. It does not replace a professionally calibrated incident light meter. Readings may be affected by the iPhone camera, automatic exposure behavior, HDR, lens choice, and environment light.

Trial and Purchase

Light Meter includes a one-day trial. After the trial, main features require a non-consumable one-time lifetime unlock through Apple StoreKit.

Privacy and Local Processing

Metering, photo capture, and video recording are processed locally on device. Light Meter does not upload camera previews, photos, videos, microphone audio, location, account, or tracking data.
